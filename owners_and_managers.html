Features

Trust Your Guest

Trust-d provides the most comprehensive solution available in the market. We give you more than guest validation data. We will provide you a trust measure of the guest so that you can instantly take action.

Lower Your Insurance Cost

Insurance premiums are relatively high for risky guests. Provide the "trust measure" to your insurance to receive discounts. Separate "highly trusted" guests from the "low trusted" guests.

Smooth Operations

Organized, well behaving, established guests will have high trust scores. High trust means problem free stays, and smooth operations.

Report Guest Issues

Our Confidence Soution Additionally incorporates post stay ratings of the guest. As hosts, you help to improve the community by rating guests after their check-out.
