Introducing Trust Your Guest metric, TYG

Trust-d believes Guest Verification using only publicly available data, or acquiring data from third party services is not enough to assess risk and assure Guest Confidence.

We incorporate a new dimension of data.

Applying our patent-pending AI algorithm, we uniquely aggregate all Static and Dynamic Trust metrics of a Guest into a single Trust Confidence Score, which is actionable, secure, and private. It is a Trust Your Guest (TYG) measure, and goes much beyond a simple web verification. Our partners can integrate TYG into their system and automatize their process.

Is Guest Verification Enough?

Verification is the first and easiest step in risk assurance, however, it is not enough in Today's ever growing demand in the Sharing Economy. Name resolution problem and accuracy of the public data are just two of many issues in verification. We believe a more robust, cross-vertical, actionable, trendy, trust metric is an inevitability. We bring Community Trust, Professional Trust, Financial Trust, and Sectoral Trust on top of verification.

Professional

Each guest has a professional life which contributes positively to Guest Verification process. We are incorporating Professional Trust measure in to our system.

Community

Each guest lives in a community which is an essential part of life. Community endorsement will contribute positively to Guest Trust Measure.

Financial

Credit score is nothing more than a "Financial Trust". Guests providing their credit score will receive points in their favor to prove their trust measure.

Sectoral

Our system treats post-stay ratings as an important parameter towards building trust measure. We incorporate post-stay ratings into our model.

Verification

Based on guest parameters, we first conduct a search throughout publicly available data, mostly Global Watch Lists, fraud databases, Interpol Lists, Sex Offender Lists, suspicious IPs, Criminal Lists, etc. Verification result is just an input to our evaluation process. We provide a short report to our partners, showing Trust Confidence Score and the result of verification.
